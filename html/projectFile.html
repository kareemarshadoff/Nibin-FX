HUMANS BALLOON CITY

Final Out

Breakdown

I am excited to show my final project from my Master's degree in Digital Effects course at Bournemouth University, which me and my classmate Nithin Seshadri created together. Working on this project was a good learning experience that has improved my skills. My contribution in this project were Human Inflation FX, Cloth Simulation, Flying Balloon FX, pop FX, Hair grooming, Tracking, Dummy Geo, Human Modeling, Photogrametry, Rigging , Animation, Lighting and Compositing. This project involved using Houdini, Nuke, PFtrack, Photoshop, ZBrush, Wrap, and Agisoft (Metashape).
